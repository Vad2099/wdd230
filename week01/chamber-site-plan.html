Gustavo A. Madero Chamber of Commerce

Fostering economic and business growth in the Gustavo A. Madero region

Site Purpose

The purpose of this site is to serve as an economic catalyst, providing resources and support for local businesses in the Gustavo A. Madero area. The site will offer information on services, events, and community resources to enhance business networking and economic growth.

Scenarios

  • How can I join the Gustavo A. Madero Chamber of Commerce?
  • What upcoming events are available for local business owners?
  • Where can I find funding opportunities for small businesses?

Color Scheme

The site will use the following color palette:

  • Primary Color: #003D7A (used for headers and navigation bar background)
  • Secondary Color: #FFC31F (used for buttons and call-to-action elements)
  • Complementary Color: #FF9A5C (used for interactive elements and highlights)
  • Background Color: #F5F5F5 (used for the main page background to improve readability)

Typography

The following fonts will be used:

  • Main Font: Merriweather (for headings, emphasizing professionalism and formality)
  • Body Font: Lato (for body text, providing modern and easy-to-read content)
