Local Attractions

Visit the Chiquihuite Hill for beautiful hikes and panoramic views of the city. The Cerro del Chiquihuite is a prominent mountain located in the north of Mexico City, offering stunning panoramic views of the surrounding areas.

The Basilica of Our Lady of Guadalupe, located in the northern part of Mexico City, is one of the most visited Catholic pilgrimage sites in the world. Every year, millions of faithful make the journey to this sacred site to honor the Virgin of Guadalupe, the patron saint of Mexico.
